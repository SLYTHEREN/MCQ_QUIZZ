The following are the rules for MCQ Round

  • Each Question will be timed. A running timer of 30 seconds is shown while the participant answers the question.
  • If a participant fails to register an answer within the allotted time, they will receive no point and the round continues with next question.
  • 10 points are given for a correct answer
  • Negative 5 points are given for incorrect answers
  • The participants can give up on the question and the round will continue with next question
  • For any technical problems during the MCQ round it will be managed by the organizing team.
